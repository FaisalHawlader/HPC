YoGoKo Smart Middleware

    Middleware services

    Network

    • V2X Service - Provides protocol support for V2X communications protocols (GN/BTP, IPv6)
    • Machine To Machine Service (M2MService) - Provides protocol support for Machine to Machine and telemetry protocols (MQTT, AMQP)

    Facilities

    • Position, Velocity and Time (Pvt) - Manages the position, velocity and time informations of the ITS Station
    • vdpservice - Manages data of the ITS Station
    • Cooperative Awareness Message Service (CaService) - Implements generation, emission and reception of CAM messages
    • Decentralized Environmental Notification Basic Service (DenService) - Implements generation, emission and reception of DENM messages
    • Infrastructure to Vehicle Information Service (IviService) - Implements generation, emission and reception of IVIM messages
    • Signal Phase and Time Service (SpatService) And Map Service - Implements generation, emission and reception of Spat and Map messages
    • DSRC Frame Message Service (FrameService) - Implements emission and reception of Frame messages
    • Basic Safety Message Service (BsService) - Implements generation of BSM messages
    • Local Dynamic Map (LdmService) - Implements the LDM (Local Dynamic Map) of the ITS Station
    • (Deprecated) Position Provider (PositionProvider) - Legacy Managment the position, velocity and time informations of the ITS Station

    Application

    • Denm Triggering Application - Application for triggering DENM messages from defined use cases
    • Traffic Light Application - Application for simulating a TrafficLight

    Management

    • Station Identity manager - Management of Station identity and C-ITS security
    • Radio Management for V2X communications - Management of radio interfaces for V2X communications

    Security

    • YoGoKo Security Client - Connects YoGoKo Middleware to C-ITS security components
    • Preserve Client - Connects YoGoKo Middleware to the PRESERVE V2X Security Subsystem (VSS)

    Utilities

    • Record/Replay - Records/replay data received and sent by the ITS Station

    Note: Some services are not documented yet. Their documentation will come in a future version.
