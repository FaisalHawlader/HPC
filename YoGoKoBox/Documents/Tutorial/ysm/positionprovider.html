    (Deprecated) Position Provider (PositionProvider)

    This service is deprecated and has been replaced by the Position, Velocity and Time (Pvt).

    The Position Provideris the legacy service which manages position, velocity and time information of the station.

    It can manage sources of different types (GNSS source, static positioning, etc).
